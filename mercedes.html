About Mercedes

Mercedes-Benz, often simply referred to as Mercedes, is one of the most iconic and respected car manufacturers in the world. Founded in 1926, the company has its roots in the invention of the first gasoline-powered automobile by Karl Benz in 1886.

The brand is renowned for producing vehicles that combine elegance, luxury, and cutting-edge technology. From executive sedans to high-performance sports cars and innovative electric vehicles, Mercedes-Benz has consistently set standards in design, comfort, and safety.

Mercedes' S-Class has long been considered a benchmark for luxury vehicles, introducing pioneering features that later become standard across the automotive industry. Meanwhile, the AMG performance division caters to enthusiasts seeking speed and precision engineering.
