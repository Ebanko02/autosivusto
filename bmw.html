About BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, better known as BMW, is a German luxury vehicle manufacturer founded in 1916. Known for its performance, innovation, and precision engineering, BMW continues to be one of the most respected automotive brands worldwide.

Originally a manufacturer of aircraft engines, BMW transitioned to motorcycles and eventually to cars. Today, BMW is synonymous with dynamic driving, cutting-edge technology, and elegant design. The brand slogan "The Ultimate Driving Machine" reflects its commitment to delivering sheer driving pleasure.
