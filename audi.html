About Audi

Audi AG is a German automobile manufacturer that designs, engineers, produces, and distributes luxury vehicles. Founded in 1909 by August Horch and re-established as Audi in 1932, the company is headquartered in Ingolstadt, Bavaria. Audi is a member of the Volkswagen Group and is known for its advanced engineering, refined design, and premium driving experience.

Today, Audi continues to push boundaries in performance and sustainability through its high-performance RS models and expanding line of electric vehicles like the Audi e-tron. The brand’s slogan, “Vorsprung durch Technik” ("Advancement through Technology"), embodies its commitment to progress and innovation in every vehicle.
